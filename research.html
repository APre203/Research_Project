Research Summary

In the Yu lab, we use study how protein arginine methylation regulates various facets of cellular processes in eukaryotes, using both the budding yeast and mammalian cells as our model organism. We take an interdisciplinary approach to investigate the role of this post- translational modification in transcriptional regulation, pre-mRNA processing events, and signaling. We are also interested in learning how these methylation-dependent cellular controls go away in disease.
